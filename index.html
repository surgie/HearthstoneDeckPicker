Epsilon-greedy Explorer

Epsilon-greedy is a strategy to resolve the Multi-armed bandit problem. Essentially when trying to find the best option from a list of items, we want to split our time between exploring options and exploiting the current best option.

The value of Epsilon will control this ratio, where a higher value will encourage more exploration and lower will encourage more exploitation of the current best option (e.g. An epsilon of 90% would choose a random option 90% of the time and exploit best option 10% of the time.)
